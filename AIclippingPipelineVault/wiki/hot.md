Don't toggle thinking ON in LM Studio for the clip model. — [[concepts/bugs-and-fixes#BUG 67]]
- **Double-launch now GUARDED (BUG 67 fixed)** — `is_pipeline_running()` checks the on-disk `pipeline.pid` marker cross-process, so a second/restarted dashboard's play returns 409 and `api_stop` kills via the marker.
